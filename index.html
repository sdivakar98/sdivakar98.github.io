01 — About

The background & the pivot

Why AI governance?

AI governance needs people who can look at a model and ask the same questions a forensic auditor would — what can go wrong, who is affected, and can you prove it's working as intended?

After 5+ years building those skills in financial investigations and external audit at EY and the Office of the Auditor General, I'm applying them to the fast-growing challenge of keeping AI systems accountable.

My combination of technical data science knowledge, forensic investigation experience, and deep familiarity with compliance frameworks is rare in this space — and that's the point.
